<h2 style="margin: 0.25in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; break-after: avoid; font-size: 18pt; font-family: 'Arial Black', sans-serif; font-weight: normal;"><a name="_Toc141194178"></a>What is ServiceNow?</h2>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">ServiceNow is a web-based platform that helps us build, automate, and manage business workflows.</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">ServiceNow has two faces:</p>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0in; margin-top: 0px;">
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 0in 0px; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><a style="color: #0563c1; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://eversana.service-now.com/sp"><strong>User-facing portal (front-end):</strong></a><strong> </strong>This side of the platform gives everyone at EVERSANA a way to get IT support, including application and technology requests, issues support, and access to our knowledge base.<br /><br /></li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt 0px; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><a style="color: #0563c1; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://eversana.service-now.com"><strong>Support-facing (back-end):</strong></a> IT uses this side as our IT Service Management (ITSM) solution, helping us process all IT changes, incidents and requests.</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="margin: 0.25in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; break-after: avoid; font-size: 18pt; font-family: 'Arial Black', sans-serif; font-weight: normal;"><a name="_Toc141194179"></a>What is ITSM/ITIL?</h2>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">IT Service Management (ITSM) is <strong>the discipline of providing IT services </strong>to an organization, like EVERSANA. The Information Technology Information Library (ITIL) is <strong>a framework of best practices</strong> that help us deliver IT services that align with our business&rsquo; needs. They work together like this:</p>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0in; margin-top: 0px;">
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 0in 0px; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">ITSM lets us manage all IT services.</li>
<li style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt 0px; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">ITIL practices help us make our service delivery workflows more effective.</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="margin: 0.25in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; break-after: avoid; font-size: 18pt; font-family: 'Arial Black', sans-serif; font-weight: normal;"><a name="_Toc141194180"></a>What does a &ldquo;fulfiller&rdquo; do?</h2>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">You are responsible for delivering (fulfilling) certain IT services, including, but not limited to, opening, modifying, and closing various types of requests, incidents, and changes within ServiceNow. Your actual duties and access rights are associated with the support group(s) you belong to.</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 12pt; line-height: 150%; font-size: 14pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">You&rsquo;ll <strong>use the support-facing back-end of ServiceNow</strong> as your main tool, and you&rsquo;ll perform your role according to ITIL practices. This means you&rsquo;ll need resources and training.</p>
